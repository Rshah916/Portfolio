A little more about me

I care about the point where a good idea becomes easy to use.

My work moves between apps, websites, prototypes and design systems. I enjoy understanding why people hesitate, where a flow becomes confusing, and how thoughtful interaction can make the whole experience feel natural.

I’m constantly expanding my toolkit with Figma, Framer, Adobe Creative Suite and front-end experiments. Beyond coursework, I explore gamification and interactive design—using digital and social play to create experiences that are engaging without losing their purpose.
